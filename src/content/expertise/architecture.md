## From first sketch to buildable detail

We shape each project through a clear design process: site reading, concept development, spatial planning, material direction, approvals support, and coordinated construction drawings. The goal is not only a strong image, but a building that works in Kerala's light, rain, heat, and social life.

## What the service includes

- Site analysis, zoning, and planning strategy
- Concept design and design development
- Floor plans, elevations, sections, and working drawings
- Material, facade, and spatial character direction
- Coordination with consultants, contractors, and site teams

Our architectural work is intentionally practical. Every drawing is prepared with construction, maintenance, budget, and long-term use in mind.
