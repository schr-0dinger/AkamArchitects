## Renewal without erasing character

We study the existing structure before proposing changes. Light, circulation, storage, services, waterproofing, and material life are reviewed together so the renovated space feels fresh without losing its original value.

## What the service includes

- Existing-condition study and measured planning
- Layout revisions and adaptive reuse strategies
- Interior upgrades and material refreshes
- Service, lighting, and performance improvements
- Site coordination for phased execution

Renovations can be complex, but a considered design process helps make the work cleaner, more purposeful, and easier to execute.
