## Fast physical feedback for design decisions

We use 3D printing to study ideas before committing to full-scale fabrication. It is useful for massing models, product prototypes, joinery studies, facade details, and client presentations where a physical object can explain more than a drawing.

## What the service includes

- 3D model preparation for print
- Prototype prints for product and detail studies
- Architectural components and presentation pieces
- Iteration support after review
- Coordination between digital files and physical output

The process helps teams make better decisions earlier, with less guesswork around scale, proportion, and form.
