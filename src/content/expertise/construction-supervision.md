## Design intent carried through execution

Good construction depends on sequencing, communication, and careful checking. We visit the site, review workmanship, clarify drawings, and coordinate with the execution team so the built result stays aligned with the approved design.

## What the service includes

- Periodic site inspections and progress reviews
- Drawing clarifications for contractors and vendors
- Material and finish checks
- Coordination across civil, interior, lighting, and services work
- Snagging support before handover

This service helps reduce rework, preserve detailing quality, and keep construction decisions accountable to the larger design.
