## Designed objects for designed spaces

We develop custom products and built elements when off-the-shelf solutions do not fit the brief. The work can include furniture, lighting objects, handles, display pieces, modular systems, and other details that need a considered design response.

## What the service includes

- Product concepts and design development
- Material, finish, and fabrication strategy
- Ergonomic and use-case refinement
- Vendor coordination and prototype review
- Integration with interiors and architectural detailing

This service is useful for clients who want the smaller elements of a project to feel as resolved as the larger spaces.
