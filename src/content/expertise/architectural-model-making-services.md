## Physical models for clearer conversations

We create models that support the design process and communicate the project with precision. Depending on the need, models can be simple massing studies, detailed presentation pieces, or site-focused studies that show context and scale.

## What the service includes

- Concept and massing models
- Presentation models for clients and stakeholders
- Site and context models
- Material and finish studies for model output
- Coordination from drawings or digital 3D files

Model-making helps align everyone around the same spatial understanding before major project decisions are locked.
