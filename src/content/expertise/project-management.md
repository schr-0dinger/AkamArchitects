## Clarity through the full project cycle

Building involves many decisions and many teams. We help structure the process so drawings, procurement, site work, and approvals are coordinated before small issues become expensive delays.

## What the service includes

- Scope definition, phasing, and milestone planning
- Consultant and contractor coordination
- Budget tracking and decision support
- Site meeting documentation and follow-up
- Quality, timeline, and handover coordination

The service is especially useful for clients who want design leadership and practical oversight without managing every detail themselves.
