description: >
  Villa Serein was designed for a couple downsizing from a much larger family
  home, and the

  brief was about calm rather than scale. A single-storey plan wraps around a central

  reflecting pool, so every principal room - living, dining, and both bedrooms - opens onto

  water and greenery.


  Pale limestone, wide sliding glass, and deep verandahs keep the interior cool without

  relying heavily on air conditioning, while the material palette stays deliberately quiet so

  the couple's art collection remains the focal point.
