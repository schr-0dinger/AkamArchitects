description: >
  Set along a quiet stretch of Kerala's backwaters, Cape Shore Resort was
  planned to feel

  discovered rather than built - a loose cluster of villas threaded through existing coconut

  groves, each one turned to catch a private water view. Locally sourced laterite, reclaimed

  timber, and traditional sloped roofs root the architecture in its region while the planning

  and detailing are unmistakably contemporary.


  Public spaces - the lobby pavilion, spa, and dining deck - sit lightly on the water's edge,

  designed to disappear into the landscape rather than compete with it.
