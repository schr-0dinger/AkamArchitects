description: >
  Loft Eleven occupies the top floor of a converted warehouse, and the interior
  design brief

  was to soften an industrial shell without erasing its character. Exposed services, steel

  beams, and polished concrete floors stay visible, layered against fluted oak joinery, linen

  upholstery, and a considered lighting plan that shifts from bright and functional by day to

  soft and gallery-like by night.


  Every piece of built-in furniture was custom-designed for the space, from the kitchen island

  to the mezzanine bookcase, so the loft feels tailored rather than furnished.
