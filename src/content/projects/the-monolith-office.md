excerpt: A seven-storey workplace conceived as a single sculpted mass, tempered

description: >
  For a growing technology firm, the brief called for a headquarters that reads
  as one

  confident gesture from every approach angle, rather than a stack of generic floor plates.

  The Monolith Office responds with a sculpted concrete form - deep vertical fins shade the

  glazing through the day, cutting solar gain while giving the facade its rhythm.


  Inside, column-free floor plates allow the workplace to be reconfigured as the company

  scales, while a landscaped podium and rooftop garden give staff a genuine outdoor reprieve

  in a dense commercial district.
