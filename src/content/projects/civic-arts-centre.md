description: >
  Commissioned by a state cultural trust, the Civic Arts Centre gives the city a
  single

  address for exhibitions, performance, and public gathering. A folded concrete roof spans

  the main exhibition hall column-free, admitting north light through a continuous clerestory

  while keeping direct sun off the artwork below.


  The building steps down toward an open plaza and amphitheatre, blurring the line between

  the institution and the street so the centre reads as public space first, building second.
